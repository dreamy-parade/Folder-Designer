How to use:
  • Click the color boxes to change folder and icon colors.
  • Click "Choose File" to upload an image to place on the folder.
  • Click an icon from the grid to add it to the folder.
  • Drag the image or icon to reposition it.
  • Use mouse wheel over the image or icon to resize it.
  • For touch devices, pinch to zoom the image or icon.
  • Click "Apply SVG" to use a custom SVG icon (must include <svg> tag).
  • Click "Export as PNG" to download the folder as a PNG image.
  • Click "Export as ICO" to download the folder as an ICO file.
